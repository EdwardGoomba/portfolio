A Little About Me

I'm a self taught web designer and developer based in Lancaster. I am passionate about building visually stunning and responsive pages and apps and am currently looking for opportunities to build web and mobile applications.

I first became interested in web development in high school, working on updating and editing many free game and comic websites. Since then I have rediscovered my passion for coding and have been working on a variety of projects ranging from restaurant mockups to developing new pages for apps and businesses.

When I am not working on web design I enjoy working out, long walks with my family, eating and reading.
